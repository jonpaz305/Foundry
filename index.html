Property, strategy, tax basis. Determines which engine runs.

Comparable Sales

Operating Assumptions

Vacancy + opex defaults. Applied to stabilized EGI.

Capital Structure

Acquisition, initial debt, and (BRRRR) refinance terms.

Market Analysis

Demographics (Census), SAFMR (HUD), vacancy + absorption (Census-derived).
Market Analysis ships in M4. Will populate from Census ACS and HUD FMR APIs server-side with shared caching, identical pattern to Tranche's AMI cache.

Risk Register

Auto-flagged risks based on engine outputs and market data.
Risk Register ships in M5. Mode-aware templates: BRRRR will flag low DSCR, high LTV at refi, insufficient cash-out, breakeven occupancy above market. F&F will flag comp scatter, high DOM, thin value creation %, contingency below 5% of project cost.

Reports

Institutional-grade output for lenders, equity partners, and IC.
Reports ship in M6. Six report types planned: Deal Snapshot, BRRRR Underwriting Package, Fix & Flip Underwriting Package, Internal Deal Memo (mode-aware), IC Memo, and Lender Package. PDF export reuses Tranche's print pipeline.
